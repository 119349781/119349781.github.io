Sport

Sport is one of my main interests. Since a young age I have always been heavily involved in all different types of sport. From tennis to Gaelic football, basketball to hurling, there are very few sports I haven't tried.
Recently kick-boxing has a big interest for me. I started kick-boxing when I was 13 and was immediately hooked. There's such an exhilarating rush from training and then fighting. It also offers the opportunity to visit other countries, my trainer has been all over the world!
Another interest of mine is soccer. Liverpool is my favourite team. They have been my dads favourite team since he was a child so naturally I followed in his footsteps. We go over occasionally to watch the matches, it always makes for a fun weekend.
Gaelic football was a big part of my life when I was growing up. I began playing in under 8's and played right up until I was 16. We always went to watch the Cork team playing matches from Parc Ui Chaoimh to Croke Park.

Travel

Travelling is one of my favourite tings to do. I love going to different countries and experiences different cultures, or even to just go and relax in the sun. I've travelled to many different cities such as Paris, London, Marbella; but by far my favourite place to visit was New York City.
I went to New York City in August 2019 and it was amazing! I saw loads of tourist attractions such as:

  • Central Park
  • The Statue of Liberty
  • Brooklyn Bridge
  • Times Square
  • The Empire State Building
  • The 9/11 Memorial

We spent 5 days there. The hotel we stayed at was very central on 6th Avenue. They had amazing food there too! I loved getting breakfast every morning, there was a nice restaurant beside our hotel who had the nicest chocolate chip pancakes but strawberries and maple syrup, it was to die for!

Paris was another city that I loved, we went there for my mom's birthday. The food there was amazing, I loved all the pastries there especially the pain au chocolats. We saw lots of tourists attractions there too, for example:

  1. The Eiffel Tower
  2. The Louvre
  3. The l'Arc de Triomphe
  4. The Champs Elysees
  5. Disneyland Paris

Disney was by far the highlight of the trip. I loved going on all the rides there my favourite was the Aerosmith Rollercoaster. The lights show at the end of the night was spectacular, definitely a must see for anyone visiting Paris.
